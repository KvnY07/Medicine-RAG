# 药物生产规范检查清单问题生成器

## 关键点：

Standard Operating Procedures for Hygiene Control 卫生控制的标准操作程序
The Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for Hygiene Control should include the following key components: Procedures for monitoring and following up on the health management and conditions of the personnel, ensuring no infectious diseases or health risks are present. Hygiene instructions should be provided to working personnel, including handwashing and disinfection guidelines, to prevent contamination of products. Specifications for clothing, laundry, and gowning must be clearly defined to ensure all personnel wear appropriate attire that meets hygiene requirements. The cleaning method for work areas, including disinfection when necessary, and the cleaning frequency should be well-established to maintain a dust- and germ-free environment. The chemicals and instruments used for cleaning work areas must meet hygiene standards to ensure they are safe and effective. Methods for evaluating cleanliness should be implemented and regularly checked to ensure they meet set standards. Hygiene control guidelines specific to the production area must be documented and followed, with instructions for cleaning production facilities and evaluating cleanliness effectiveness to ensure no contamination risks. Additionally, designated personnel should be responsible for cleanliness, with clear cleaning and disinfection programs, methods, and tools used in cleaning. The disassembly and assembly methods of production equipment should be standardized to ensure hygiene is not compromised, and the removal of marks from previous operations should be carried out to prevent cross-contamination. Cleanliness should be maintained throughout production, and cleanliness checks must be performed before operation starts. Pest control measures should be in place, with regular checks to prevent any pest intrusion. Lastly, other essential matters, such as the responsibilities of the production department head, should also be specified to ensure proper implementation of hygiene management and ensure product quality and production safety.
